Inspiring and recognizing FIRST ® Tech Challenge students in Orlando, FL

Every year, students in the Orlando area use their skills to build, program, and compete with robots. We work to inspire and celebrate their accomplishments through training and tournaments.

A global robotics community preparing young people for the future. FIRST has programs for various age groups.

Teams (grades 7-12) are challenged to design, build, program, and operate robots to compete in a head-to-head challenge in an alliance format.

Florida teams compete in local leagues to qualify for the Florida Championship, where they can then qualify for a world Championship event.

Tesla Robotics League operates meets and a League Tournament event for teams in the Orlando area.

News & Announcements

Official announcements, including pre-event emails for teams and volunteers, are delivered using a Google Group. Subscribe to the group to stay up-to-date. There are two ways to subscribe:

  1. Visit the group and sign in with a Google account (it doesn't have to be an @gmail.com address). Then join the group.
  2. Send an email to the group. When the system sends an automated response, reply one more time to confirm your subscription.
